Warning Signs

Signs Your Amazon Ads Are Underperforming

Most of these problems are invisible in standard reporting. They only show up when you look at the right data.

01

ACoS improves but organic rankings don't move

Rising Costs, No Rank Improvement

Your agency optimizes ACoS without tracking what it costs your organic rank. Cutting bids reduces spend but also reduces the sales velocity that builds organic position.

02

Budget consumed by irrelevant search terms

Search Term Bleed

Your budget is funding searches that will never convert. Without weekly harvesting and a tight negative keyword protocol, campaigns quietly drain spend on irrelevant queries.

03

Your ads are reaching existing customers more than new ones

Low New-to-Brand Rate

Your campaigns are not acquiring new customers at the rate they should. Most of your ad sales go to repeat buyers while your category share stays flat.

04

ACoS looks fine but TACoS is 20%+

TACoS Above Benchmark

Ads are carrying all the revenue load because organic rank isn't growing alongside them. The two strategies are running in parallel, not compounding.

05

Campaigns were set up once and never restructured

No Campaign Architecture

Budget flows to your highest volume keywords and your most profitable targets never get funded. Without BCG separation and match type structure, spend concentrates where it is least efficient.

06

Ads and SEO managed by different teams targeting different keywords

Siloed Strategy

Your ad spend builds revenue but not organic rank. The flywheel never starts because neither strategy knows what the other is doing.

FAQ

Common Questions About Amazon PPC Management

What does an Amazon advertising agency do?

+

Manages and optimises your complete Amazon ads stack — Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, Sponsored Display, and DSP campaigns. Connects advertising strategy to organic SEO, listing quality, and overall profitability metrics like TACoS.

Who is this Amazon advertising service for?

+

Brands already selling on Amazon that want to scale profitably — particularly those spending $5,000+ per month on ads, managing 50+ SKUs, or struggling with rising ACoS and TACoS despite growing ad spend.

How is Growisto different from other Amazon PPC agencies?

+

Maximum 2–3 team members per account, we optimise for TACoS and profitability not just ACoS and revenue, and connect advertising strategy to organic SEO and listing quality so campaigns compound rather than cannibalize organic rank.

Do you manage all types of Amazon ads including DSP?

+

Yes. Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands (video and non-video), Sponsored Display, Amazon DSP (upper-funnel programmatic), and Amazon Marketing Cloud (AMC) for attribution and audience analysis.

How do you measure success in Amazon advertising?

+

TACoS (Total ACoS) as the primary profitability metric. We also track new-to-brand customer acquisition rate, contribution margin per ASIN, and organic rank movement alongside paid metrics.

What kind of results can I expect and in what timeframe?

+

Initial improvements in campaign structure and wasted spend are typically visible within the first 4–6 weeks. Meaningful TACoS improvement and organic rank gains usually appear within 90 days.

How much does Amazon advertising cost?

+

Scoped per account based on ad spend under management, number of ASINs, and the breadth of services required. We don't use fixed percentage-of-spend models — book a discovery call for a specific scope.

Can you reduce ad spend while increasing performance?

+

Yes — in many accounts we find significant wasted spend in the first audit. Common issues include over-matched broad keywords, uncontrolled product targeting, and campaigns without negative keyword protocols.

Do you handle keyword research and campaign optimization?

+

Yes. Keyword research combines search volume data, Amazon search query data, Brand Analytics search frequency rank, and buyer sentiment analysis from reviews and Q&As.

How does onboarding work with your Amazon advertising agency?

+

Onboarding starts with a full account audit covering campaign structure, keyword coverage, TACoS trends, wasted spend, and competitive benchmarks. We begin execution within the first 2 weeks.

Do you support multiple marketplaces and global campaigns?

+

Yes. We manage Amazon advertising across US, UK, India, and Germany marketplaces. Each marketplace gets a dedicated strategy, not a copy-paste of your primary market setup.

Is PPC better than SEO on Amazon?

+

Neither is better — they compound each other. Ad-driven sales velocity is a direct A9 ranking signal. Higher organic rank reduces dependence on paid traffic and lowers TACoS over time.

What is Alexa for Shopping and how does it affect Amazon advertising?

+

Amazon's AI shopping assistant surfaces products through conversational queries, not just keyword search. We build Alexa for Shopping readiness into every campaign structure — benefit-first copy, complete attributes, and Q&A content structured for AI citation.
